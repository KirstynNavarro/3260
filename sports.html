About the Team

Origin: The Savannah, Georgia team was founded in 2016 by Jesse and Emily Cole, the team originally played in the Coastal Plain League (CPL) before becoming a traveling, exhibition-only team in 2023. The "Fans First" Mission: The team, owned by Fans First Entertainment, focuses entirely on fan engagement, often removing advertisements, selling tickets with food included, and fostering a "circus" atmosphere. For the 2026 season, the Savannah Bananas are led by Head Coach Tyler Gillum and Assistant Coach/Director of Player Development Adam Virant, with Ray Ortega. History of Success: Before switching to exhibition, they won three CPL Petitt Cup championships (2016, 2021, 2022).
